Welcome To Kitengela Adventist School

Welcome to Kitengela Adventist School, a place where young minds are nurtured, values are instilled, and futures are shaped with purpose. We are a Christian-based institution committed to providing a high-quality, holistic education that empowers our learners academically, spiritually, socially, and physically.our school offers a nurturing environment whereeach child is encouraged to grow in character, faith, and knowledge.

At Kitengela Adventist School, we believe that every child is unique and full of potential. Our dedicated team of qualified and caring teachers work closely with learners to ensure that they receive individual attention, guidance, and support throughout their academic journey. We blend a strong curriculum with co-curricular activities, life skills training, and spiritual guidance to prepare students not only for exams but also for life beyond the classroom.

We are more than just a school—we are a family that values respect, integrity, responsibility, and excellence. Whether you're a parent seeking the best start for your child or a visitor looking to learn more about us, we warmly invite you to explore what makes Kitengela Adventist School a special place to learn, grow, and thrive.

Growing Beyond The Classroom

At Kitengela Adventist School, we are committed to nurturing learners not only academically but also socially, physically, and spiritually through a vibrant and well-structured co-curricular program. We recognize that every child is gifted uniquely, and we provide a platform where talents are discovered, nurtured, and celebrated. Our Pathfinder Club instills discipline, leadership, and a deep sense of service among our learners, while our active Scouts movement fosters teamwork, resilience, and a love for the outdoors.

Our school choir continues to set high standards through its inspiring performances, both in school and during external events. We are especially proud of our enthusiastic participation in national and regional music festivals, where our learners showcase their vocal, instrumental, and cultural talents while gaining confidence and stage experience. These festivals not only promote artistic expression but also help students appreciate Kenya’s rich cultural diversity.

In the field of sports, our pupils excel in football, athletics, and other games that promote physical fitness, discipline, and teamwork. Academic clubs such as debate and public speaking sharpen their communication and critical thinking skills, while drama and creative arts provide outlets for expression and imagination.

Through all these activities, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are confident, respectful, and ready to contribute positively to society. At Kitengela Adventist School, every co-curricular activity is an opportunity to grow, lead, and shine.

Academic Stages – CBC Curriculum

Early Years Education (Pre-Primary 1 & 2)

Ages: 4–5 years

Focus on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, spiritual growth, social skills, and physical development through play-based learning.

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

Ages: 6–8 years

Core learning in literacy, Kiswahili, mathematics, religious education, environmental activities, and life skills. Emphasis on nurturing curiosity and moral responsibility.

Middle Primary (Grades 4–6)

Ages: 9–11 years

Learners explore a broader curriculum including science, home science, agriculture, creative arts, and technology. Evaluation includes classroom-based assessments and practical activities.

Junior Secondary School (Grades 7–9)

Ages: 12–14 years

A critical stage that prepares learners for senior school and future careers. Subjects offered include:

  • Integrated Science
  • Mathematics
  • Christian Religious Education
  • Business Studies
  • Pre-Technical Studies
  • Health Education
  • ICT, Sports, and Life Skills

We guide learners through career pathways and talent development while upholding Adventist principles and academic excellence.
