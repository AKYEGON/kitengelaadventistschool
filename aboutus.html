About Our School

Our school is dedicated to providing excellent education and nurturing young minds to thrive in a global world.

Our Vision

To be a christian institution of academic excellence.

Our Mission

To prepare learners for responsible citizenship by holistic education that fosters a balanced of aperson for service to God and humanity.

Core Values

Integrity

We instill honesty, responsibility, and respect in all our learners.

Innovation

We promote critical thinking and creativity through modern teaching techniques.

Teamwork

We foster collaboration and mutual support among staff and students.

Responsibility

We take accountability for our actions and aim to make a positive impact in society.

Respect

We treat others with kindness, understanding, and appreciation for their unique contributions.

Perserverence

We foster resilience and determination in the face of challenges.

“Rooted in Christ, Rising in Excellence.” — “Discipline + Dedication = Destiny” — “Every Child is a Star.”
